Rules

To Sell a Product

1. You have to enter all the product details asked.
2. You will get a product ID after posting your item.
3. Remember product entered must be in working condition.
4. Do not enter false image else you will be banned from server.


Registration of Product

1. You have to register yourself for bidding of product.
2. You can find the desired product by selecting the category of product.
3. Maximum participants for registration of particular product are 10.


Join Bid

1. To join big you need enter the product ID.
2. The total time of bid will be 10 minutes.
3. Time for particular bid will be 25 seconds if any user makes a higher bid within those 25 seconds then 25 seconds counter will be again started else the person with last bid will be winner.
4. If there will be no winner then after end of total time the person with last bid will be winner.
5. Remember if you are placing first bid then its amount must be greater than or equal to base price.
6. You cannot place bid consecutively.
7. Your bid must be greater than previous bid.
